About Lucintis

Lucintis is an intelligence technology company focused on forecasting geopolitical conflict, civil unrest, and political instability using machine learning and dynamic exposure modeling. Purpose-built for decision-makers in finance, insurance, defense, and global enterprise, Lucintis delivers interpretable, forward-looking forecasts that translate real-world risk into actionable insight.

Our Mission

To equip global decision-makers with clear intelligence on political instability and conflict—transforming uncertainty into strategic foresight.

Why Lucintis?

The name Lucintis is derived from “luc” (light/clarity) and “intis” (intelligence). It captures our central promise: clarity through intelligence.
